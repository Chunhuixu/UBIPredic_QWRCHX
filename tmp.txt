CC   -!- FUNCTION: Initiates apoptosis in a BH3-independent mechanism
CC       possibly by recruiting ced-3 to mitochondria and other cytoplasmic
CC       membranes (PubMed:11114722). Has a role in lifespan and tumor
CC       growth (PubMed:16380712, PubMed:17934462). Required for the
CC       induction of mitophagy under stress conditions (PubMed:25896323).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11114722, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16380712,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17934462, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25896323}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer; via transmembrane domain (PubMed:11114722,
CC       PubMed:9824163). Interacts with ced-3 and ced-9 (PubMed:9824163).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11114722, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9824163}.

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11114722, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25896323}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: RNAi-mediated knockdown causes an increased
CC       sensitivity to stress and inhibits mitophagy which leads to an
CC       accumulation of defective mitochondria that have increased mass,
CC       altered network morphology, decreased ATP levels, increased
CC       reactive oxygen species (ROS) generation, membrane depolarization,
CC       increased oxygen consumption and increased cytoplasmic Ca(2+) that
CC       intensifies under stress conditions. RNAi-mediated knockdown in
CC       daf-2, isp-1, or clk-1 mutant backgrounds suppresses their
CC       increased lifespan phenotype. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25896323}.
